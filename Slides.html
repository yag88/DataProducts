Our Subject: Survival during the sinking of the Titanic

Among the datasets included in Base R, there is the statistics of survival for the 2224 passengers on the Titanic. 1502 died during the shipwreck in 1912. But the percentage of survival was very different according to class, sex and age. I used the ratio of survivors for each category as a probability of survival. A random variable with that probability decides if you survive or not
